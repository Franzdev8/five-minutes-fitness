For your body, mind and soul


Maximum fitness in the shortest amount of time

Our five minutes workouts are designed for everyone who has a tight time schedule but still wants to gain the highest possible amounts of fitness for the body and the mind, backed up by science.


Four minutes for body - one minute for mind

Your training will consist of a four minute Tabata/HIIT workout using your bodyweight followed by a one minute Meditation/Mindfullness workout.


Train anywhere at anytime you want

Since you will only be using your bodyweight for the fitness workouts you can train wherever you are during your day, at any time you want.


Daily customized workouts for your fitness level

Everyday, seven days per week, you will get the link to a new customized workout for you according to your fitness level. All workouts can be played as a video or as an audio file, whatever fits you best.
